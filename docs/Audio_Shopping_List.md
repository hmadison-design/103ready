# Cylinder Three — Audio Shopping List

Download these sounds from Freesound.org and place them in a new folder:
**CYOA/Twine Stuff/audio/**

## Required Sounds

### 1. engine-smooth.mp3
**What:** Cockpit interior of a piston single (Cessna 172/182 ideal) in steady cruise. You want the continuous hum of the engine as heard from the pilot's seat — not external flyby sounds.
**Length:** 10-30 seconds (will be looped)
**Where to look:**
- https://freesound.org/people/JLWSound/sounds/322321/ — Cessna 172E engine start/idle/takeoff (extract the cruise portion)
- https://freesound.org/people/-CASK-/sounds/624587/ — Cessna Longitude cockpit (jet, but loopable cockpit ambience)
- https://freesound.org/people/KIZILSUNGUR/packs/13004/ — Cockpit sounds pack
- Search: `cessna cockpit interior engine cruise` or `light aircraft cabin ambience`

### 2. engine-rough.mp3
**What:** Same perspective (cockpit interior) but with audible roughness — vibration, slight unevenness in the engine note. Think of a piston engine running on 5 of 6 cylinders. If you can't find this specifically, we can process engine-smooth.mp3 with amplitude modulation to create it.
**Length:** 10-30 seconds (will be looped)
**Where to look:**
- https://freesound.org/people/craigsmith/sounds/487159/ — Sputtering engine
- https://freesound.org/people/craigsmith/packs/24884/ — Vintage prop aircraft pack
- Search: `engine rough running sputter piston` or `old engine misfire`

### 3. engine-cough.mp3
**What:** A single engine miss/cough — a brief stumble where the engine drops RPM and catches itself. Short and sharp.
**Length:** 1-3 seconds
**Where to look:**
- https://freesound.org/people/se2001/sounds/469616/ — Sputtering motor
- Search: `engine cough miss sputter stall` or `engine stumble`

### 4. engine-fail.mp3
**What:** Progressive engine power loss — RPM decaying, engine sputtering, winding down. The sound of an engine giving up.
**Length:** 5-15 seconds
**Where to look:**
- https://freesound.org/people/Temp6754/sounds/535906/ — Engine stop/fail
- Search: `engine failure shutdown power loss propeller stop`

### 5. radio-squelch.mp3
**What:** Aviation radio squelch — the click/burst of static when a radio transmission begins or ends. The classic "kssht" sound.
**Length:** 0.5-1.5 seconds
**Where to look:**
- https://freesound.org/people/JovianSounds/sounds/524205/ — Radio sign off/squelch
- https://freesound.org/people/ERH/sounds/30328/ — Radio click
- https://freesound.org/people/ERH/packs/1860/ — Radio clicks and noises pack
- Search: `radio squelch click aviation` or `walkie talkie click`

### 6. wind-quiet.mp3
**What:** Wind noise as heard from inside an aircraft cockpit with the engine off — airframe buffet, wind over the canopy/wings. The eerie quiet after an engine failure.
**Length:** 10-20 seconds (will be looped)
**Where to look:**
- Search: `wind cockpit glider` or `wind aircraft interior no engine` or `glider cockpit ambience`

### 7. touchdown.mp3
**What:** Aircraft wheels touching pavement — tire screech/chirp on concrete. Single event.
**Length:** 1-3 seconds
**Where to look:**
- Search: `airplane landing wheels touchdown tire` or `plane landing gear chirp`

## Download Notes

- **Format:** MP3 preferred (smaller files, SugarCube handles it natively). WAV or OGG also work.
- **License:** Look for CC0 (public domain) or CC-BY. CC-BY-NC may cause issues if 103ready.com has any commercial element. Note the attribution requirements for CC-BY sounds — we'll add credits to the scenario.
- **Quality:** 128kbps MP3 minimum. Higher is fine but not needed for web delivery.
- **If you can't find an exact match:** Download the closest thing. I can process, trim, loop, normalize, and mix audio files from here once they're in the folder.
